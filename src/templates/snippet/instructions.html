Instructions

To generate a palette with n colors, just enter the number of colors you want and click Generate. Bigger palettes will take longer than smaller palettes to make. Results will automatically appear when ready.

You can change the appearence of palettes in several ways. The first is by changing how Colorgorical picks colors by dragging the sliders to the left. They control how important two types of discriminability (perceptual distance and color-name difference), color preference (pair preference), and color-name uniqueness should be when selecting colors.

You can restrict which hues to include in your palette by using the select hue filters panel in the menu bar. By clicking on the hue wheel, or by entering an angle interval manually, you can define which hue regions to include in your palette. Lightness of colors can be similarly filtered.

You can guarentee that certain colors are in your palette by entering them through the Add starting palette colors panel in the menu bar. Make sure that you enter colors one at a time, without quotes, and in either HEX or RGB format. Alternatively, if you generate a palette and want to add a color in it to future generations, click the start button.

For more information, please consult our paper or the documentation.
