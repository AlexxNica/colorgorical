Colorgorical

Colorgorical is an open-sourced tool to create information visualization categorical color palettes. Built explicitly for information visualization design, Colorgorical automatically creates palettes based on user-defined balance of color discriminability vs. aesthetic preference.

You can try out Colorgorical at here, and it is open sourced here.

The tool was written by Connor Gramazio with advisement from David Laidlaw and Karen Schloss.
